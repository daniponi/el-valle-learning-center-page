El Valle Learning Center

The El Valle Learning Center represents a new, innovative educational option for families seeking a high-quality private secondary education in English. Strategically located in the heart of beautiful El Valle de Antón, our center combines international academic excellence with a local community integration. El Valle Learning Center's mission is to offer a modern, progressive, and student-centered education.

Why choose El Valle Learning Center?

🎯 OUR EDUCATIONAL PROPOSAL

El Valle Learning Center combines high academic standards with a personalized approach to optimize each student's development in our collaborative, multicultural environment. Our rigorous curriculum and U.S. diploma program provide effective preparation for universities worldwide, ensuring graduates are equipped with both the academic excellence and global perspective needed for success in today's interconnected world.

what we do

El Valle Learning Center offers a comprehensive educational program designed to meet the diverse needs of our students.

🎓

U.S. HIGH SCHOOL DIPLOMA

Our graduates receive a U.S. high school diploma recognized by universities abroad and in Panama.

👥

SMALL GROUP SIZES

Personalized education approach with a maximum of 15 students per class. Our personalized education approach maintains a maximum of 15 students per class, ensuring each student receives the individual attention and support they need to thrive. and adjust their teaching in real time. The result is a collaborative environment that naturally promotes teamwork and lasting friendships among students.

📚

CORE SUBJECTS

The core subjects of Math, English, Science, and Social Studies are learned through a combination of in-person and online instruction. Our core subjects are taught in English following international standards, with a specialized support system for students who are developing their language proficiency.

🗪

LANGUAGES

Spanish as a second language classes are included for students who need to develop fluency in Spanish as well as English as second classes for studnets who need to catch up on English.

🤖 🎭 ⚽

EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Extracurricular activities develop creativity and allow students to explore their unique talents and holistic development based on their interests. We offer technology, art and cretivity, sports and actove life, hiking, cooking

👩🏻‍🏫

TEACHING STYLE

We implement a modern education approach with hands-on learning and projects that goes beyond traditional memorization and focuses on developing 21st-century skills. Our approach fosters problem-solving, collaboration, creativity, and innovation. We cultivate critical thinking through collaborative learning in a positive, trust-based school community, where academic risk-taking is celebrated as an essential part of intellectual and personal growth.
